##### Komposisi dan bumbu yang digunakan dalam pembuatan Oxtail Soup:

1. Gunakan 1 kg Oxtail
1. Sediakan 3 Nutmeg (crushed lil bit)
1. Sediakan 7 Garlic (crushed lil bit)
1. Ambil 1 Handfull White Pepper (whole)
1. Siapkan 2 Pcs Potatoes (big cut)
1. Gunakan 2 Pcs Carrots (big cut)
1. Sediakan 1 Sdm Salt
1. Gunakan 1/2 Sdm Sugar
1. Siapkan 1/2 Sdm Totole
1. Siapkan 1/2 Sdm White Pepper Powder
1. Gunakan  Celery




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Cara mengolah Oxtail Soup:

1. Boil oxtail for 2 hours. Then strain the fat. Move to clean pot.
1. Add garlic, nutmeg, whole pepper, salt, sugar, and bring to boil again about 10 mins.
1. Add in potatoes, carrots, and fix the flavour.
